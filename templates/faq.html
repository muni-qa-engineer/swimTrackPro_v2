Detailed Instructions

For Learners: You do not need a password! Simply enter your full name and mobile number on the homepage and click Login as Guest. The system will automatically recognize you if you've visited before, or create a new profile for you if you're new.

For Coaches: Click "Login/Register" at the top right, select the Coach tab, and use your secure Username and Password.

Before booking a session, you must add the person who will be swimming to your profile.
  1. Go to your Dashboard.
  2. Click the Add Swimmer button.
  3. Enter the swimmer's Name, Age, Gender, and current Skill Level.
  4. Click Save. You can add multiple family members to a single account!

Booking is fully customizable through our Book Session wizard:
  • Step 1 (Package): Choose between an Individual Package or a Monthly Package.
  • Step 2 (Schedule): Pick your Start Date and select your preferred training days. Note: Monthly packages require exactly 3 days a week.
  • Step 3 (Location & Swimmer): Enter your residential location using our Map Picker. Select the Swimmer from the dropdown.
  • Step 4 (Coach): The system will automatically filter and show Coaches available in your location. Select your preferred Coach and hit Proceed to Pay.

Once you confirm a booking, it will appear in your Bookings tab with a "Pending Payment" status.
  1. Click the Pay Now button on the booking card.
  2. You will see the Coach's UPI ID / Bank Details.
  3. Make the transfer using your preferred banking app (GPay, PhonePe, etc).
  4. Take a screenshot of the successful transaction.
  5. Upload the screenshot in our app. The Coach will review it and mark your booking as "Paid & Approved"!

If you are on an eligible package (like Monthly) and cannot attend a scheduled session, you can Skip it.
  • Go to your Dashboard or Calendar.
  • Find the upcoming session you want to miss and click Skip Session.
  • You will automatically be granted 1 Make-Up Credit.
  • Later, you can use the "Make-Up Request" button to propose a new date/time to the Coach using your credit!
