📘 Complete User Guide

Enter your name and mobile number, then click Login as Guest. Existing users will be recognized automatically.

Click Add Swimmer and enter the swimmer's details. Once saved, the swimmer will be available in the booking dropdown.

Select the swimmer, package, start date, class days, location, and number of participants. Then click Confirm Booking.

Monthly Package: Select exactly 3 class days.

Open your booking card and click Edit to update dates, location, payment status, and other details.

Click Delete on the booking card. Depending on permissions, the booking may be removed immediately or sent for admin approval.

Eligible packages allow you to skip sessions and earn make-up credits.

Select a new date using your available make-up credit and submit the request. The coach will approve or reject it.

Open the calendar to view all scheduled classes, skipped sessions, and approved make-up dates.

Read important announcements from the coach. Admin users can update the scrolling notice from the dashboard.

Fees are calculated automatically based on the selected package, number of class days, and number of participants. Discounts are applied automatically where applicable.

Contact the trainer by phone, WhatsApp, or email using the details listed above.
