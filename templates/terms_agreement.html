SwimTrackPro Coach Terms & Agreement

Version 1.0 (Audit-ready & legally compliant)

1. Acceptance of Terms

By registering as a Coach on SwimTrackPro, I confirm that I have read, understood, and voluntarily agree to comply with these Terms & Agreement. I understand that these terms govern my responsibilities while using the SwimTrackPro platform.

2. Accuracy of Information

I certify that all personal, professional, and qualification details provided during registration are true, complete, and accurate.

I agree to promptly update my profile if any information changes.

Providing false, misleading, or fraudulent information may result in suspension or permanent removal from the platform.

3. Professional Conduct

As a SwimTrackPro Coach, I agree to:

  • Conduct myself professionally at all times.
  • Treat every swimmer, parent, guardian, and fellow coach with courtesy and respect.
  • Maintain appropriate behaviour both inside and outside the swimming facility while representing SwimTrackPro.
  • Avoid discrimination, harassment, intimidation, or abusive behaviour of any kind.
4. Safety Responsibilities

I understand that swimmer safety is my highest priority. I agree to:

  • Follow all applicable pool safety rules and operational guidelines.
  • Ensure swimmers are trained according to their age, skill level, and physical ability.
  • Never leave swimmers unattended during assigned coaching sessions.
  • Immediately report unsafe pool conditions or emergencies.
  • Prioritize safety over training performance whenever necessary.
5. Coaching Ethics

I agree to:

  • Provide honest, responsible, and professional coaching.
  • Promote discipline, sportsmanship, and positive behaviour.
  • Avoid physical or verbal misconduct.
  • Never coach while under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or any substance that may impair judgment.
  • Maintain appropriate professional boundaries with swimmers and parents.
6. Attendance and Punctuality

I agree to:

  • Arrive on time for scheduled coaching sessions.
  • Notify SwimTrackPro as early as possible if I am unable to attend a scheduled session.
  • Respect assigned schedules and coaching responsibilities.
  • Maintain consistent attendance to provide reliable coaching services.
7. Responsibilities Toward Swimmers

I agree to:

  • Encourage swimmers positively.
  • Respect each swimmer’s learning pace.
  • Avoid unrealistic promises regarding performance or competition results.
  • Promote healthy training practices and injury prevention.
8. Platform Usage

I agree to use SwimTrackPro responsibly. I shall not:

  • Misuse platform features.
  • Manipulate bookings, attendance, payments, or schedules.
  • Access information belonging to other coaches without authorization.
  • Share my login credentials with others.
9. Confidentiality

I understand that I may have access to confidential information relating to swimmers, parents, and SwimTrackPro operations. I agree to:

  • Keep all personal information confidential.
  • Use platform data only for authorized coaching purposes.
  • Not disclose confidential information without permission, except where required by law.
10. Loyalty and Conflict of Interest

While working through SwimTrackPro, I agree to:

  • Act in the best interests of the swimmers and the platform.
  • Avoid actions that intentionally harm the reputation of SwimTrackPro.
  • Disclose any significant conflict of interest that may affect my coaching responsibilities.
11. Equipment and Facilities

I agree to:

  • Use pool facilities and training equipment responsibly.
  • Report damaged or unsafe equipment immediately.
  • Help maintain a clean and safe training environment.
12. Compliance with Laws and Policies

I agree to comply with all applicable local laws, swimming pool rules, child safety requirements, and SwimTrackPro operational policies.

13. Child Protection

I understand that many swimmers may be minors. I agree to:

  • Treat every child with dignity, patience, and respect.
  • Avoid any behaviour that could reasonably be considered inappropriate or unsafe.
  • Provide physical assistance only when necessary for instruction or safety.
  • Immediately report any safety concerns involving a swimmer.
14. Media and Photographs

I understand that SwimTrackPro may use photographs or videos taken during coaching activities for educational, promotional, or marketing purposes where appropriate permissions have been obtained.

15. Limitation of Responsibility

I understand that swimming is a physical activity that carries inherent risks. While I am responsible for providing safe coaching practices, I cannot guarantee the prevention of every injury or unforeseen incident. I am expected to exercise reasonable care, professional judgment, and follow established safety procedures at all times.

16. Account Suspension or Termination

SwimTrackPro reserves the right to suspend or terminate my coach account if I provide false information, violate safety requirements, engage in misconduct, repeatedly fail to fulfill coaching responsibilities, misuse the platform, or damage the reputation or integrity of SwimTrackPro.

17. Amendments

I understand that SwimTrackPro may update these Terms & Agreement from time to time. Continued use of the platform after such updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

Declaration

"I confirm that I have carefully read this Coach Terms & Agreement, understand my professional responsibilities, and agree to comply with all terms. I acknowledge that failure to comply may result in suspension or termination of my account."
